First visible item's index: {{datasource.adapter.firstVisible.$index}}

Adapter.check method could be useful when rows heights can change after rendering. By invoking Adapter.check() we ask the uiScroll to check if any of items that are in current viewport's buffer changed their size.

In this demo method doCheck is implemented to

  • change real size of items (from 15 to 25) in DOM;
  • run Adapter.check();
  • autoscroll to item that was first visible before change (optionally).
